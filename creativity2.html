About me

Inopia Foundation e. V.

First of all, I would like to introduce one of my biggest and most important projects so far - Inopia Foundation e. V. As someone who has been doing voluntary work since his youth, it has always been important to me to give something back to society and to support and encourage younger people. My friends and I founded a non-profit organization last year. Our goal was clearly to improve the quality of life of socially disadvantaged people with the UN agenda 2030 as a guideline. In particular, we pursue our goals in the areas of poverty, education and sustainability. We are approaching this step by step through our constant efforts within various programs and actions. Thereby we develop different action plans and participate in already existing projects. As can be seen below, in addition to the pictorial insights, I have also embedded our website. It is currently only available in german, however we are relaunching our website at the end of June which will also be available in english.
